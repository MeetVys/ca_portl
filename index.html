About Us


E-Cell IIT Hyderabad aims to create a platform to promote entrepreneurial spirit and discuss ideas among students and budding start-ups via different events and competitions. E-Cell IIT Hyderabad brings Campus Ambassador Program to create a network amongst various colleges across Hyderabad on a larger scale. We invite you to join this initiative and work along with us to make an idea into an innovation.







Why CA

An opportunity to expand your network in your campus and outside.


Opportunity to be part of various entrepreneurial events conducted by E-Cell IIT Hyderabad.


Improve your management and communication skills by being a part of CA IIT Hyderabad.


Get access to resources to enhance your knowledge about entrepreneurship and Management.


Certificate of appreciation from E-cell IITH at the end of the tenure.





Responsibilities

Publicize

Create awareness about E-Cell IIT Hyderabad and advertise the events and competitions conducted by E-Cell IITH.

Connect

Serve as an important connection between students of your college and the Cell.


Orchestrate

Ensure participation from your college in all events conducted by E-Cell IITH.



Promote

Gain followers and likes on social media.





Incentives

Free training programme by worth rupees 1.5 lakhs.




Free passes for all the events conducted by E-Cell IITH.




Sessions with alumni of IITH.





The top campus ambassadors will receive recommendations, certificate of excellence, and many more incentives.


Terms & Conditions

Students from colleges across the country can participate in this endeavor.


The Campus Ambassadors would be given certain points for each task with a public leader board to ensure Transparency.


All the events must be promoted according to the terms mentioned by E-Cell IIT Hyderabad.


All the events held under this program would be Supervised by E-Cell IIT Hyderabad directly.


In case of any dispute, the final decision lies with E-Cell IITH.


Any case of plagiarism or sharing confidential information would lead to disqualification or may lead to permanent ban in some cases.
